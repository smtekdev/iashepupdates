About Us

Shaping Safety, Health & Environment

IASHEP is a certified 501(c)(3) non-profit providing classroom, onsite, and online training for safety, health, and environmental professionals. Our programs meet the highest standards of quality and integrity. We are committed to equal opportunities for all, offering education and certification without discrimination and in full compliance with local, state, and federal laws.

Our Mission

Driving Change Through Knowledge

At IASHEP, we believe education is the key to building safer, healthier, and more sustainable communities. Since 2014, our non-profit has been dedicated to providing hands-on training, professional certifications, and resources that empower individuals across industries worldwide. With over 500 years of combined industry expertise, our mission is to transform knowledge into real-world impact—helping professionals grow while protecting lives.

Industries

Where IASHEP Makes an Impact

Construction

Construction is a high-risk industry with hazards like falls, machinery, heavy equipment, electrocution, and exposure to harmful substances. IASHEP provides resources to help workers and employers identify, reduce, and prevent these risks effectively.

Manufacturing

Manufacturing turns materials into products but poses risks like machinery injuries and exposure to hazardous substances.

Mining

Mining involves hazards like cave-ins, toxic gases, and dust, demanding strict safety and training to protect workers.

Environmental

The environmental industry focuses on preventing and reducing damage through pollution control, waste management, and ecosystem conservation.

Healthcare

The healthcare industry provides care, treatment, and services to improve health and quality of life, serving individuals and communities worldwide.

Oil and Gas

The oil and gas industry is essential for global energy production but comes with serious risks, including explosions, fires, toxic chemical exposure, gas leaks, and injuries from heavy machinery—making strong safety measures critical.

IASHEP is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing world-class training and certifications in safety, health, and environmental fields—empowering professionals to create safer, healthier workplaces worldwide.
